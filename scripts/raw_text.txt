Other Important information :

Candidates who have passed with grace marks, only actual marks obtained by the candidates

shall be counted. Grace marks awarded to him are not to be considered / counted.

Recognized or permitted Institutes / Colleges are to be treated similarly.

If a candidate has joined service (i.e. is in BHS) before last date of receipt of application, he

is to be considered as BHS for all purposes.

Result declared after last date of receipt of application will not be entertained. Last date of

the month on which examination was held is to be treated as date of passing.

All candidates have to produce their failure marks sheet. Unable to do so will make his

candidature as Not Eligible.

For all foreign graduates, marks awarded in MCI conducted eligibility exam (FMGE) are to

be used for calculation for all purposes. Marks of his Degree will not be considered,

Candidates having obtained higher qualification (Super specialization) in related course

are to be taken into consideration for awarding points.

Candidate who have done Post Graduate in broad speciality subject, but have applied for

S.R./ Tutor in related / allied super speciality subject are to be considered eligible for super

speciality SR ship.

a) MD Medicine candidates are to be considered eligible for S.R. in Geriatrics provided
the candidates have applied for S.R. in Geriatrics.

All candidates who have done super speciality e.g. MCH / DM directly after passing MBBS

are to be awarded 20 points (10 for PG & 10 for Super speciality) just as candidates who

have done DM / MCH after MD / MS etc.

All such candidates who are working as lecturer or Asst. Prof. (without doing SR / Tutor)

are to be considered eligible for SR / Tutor.

Candidates who have not submitted Non Creamy Layer Caste Certificate during couselling

/ documents verification are to be considered as UR candidates.

Every eligible candidate has to submit an affidavit stating that He / She has never been

appointed and completed the 3 years tenure post of SR / Tutor in applied subject.
